Emperor Penguins

Some facts about the Emperor Penguin are:

  • They are the tallest and biggest type of penguin
  • They live at the coasts of Anarctica.
  • They can survive and thrive in the most desolate tempratures and weather.
  • They feed off fish from the ocean
  • They raise their young in nests
